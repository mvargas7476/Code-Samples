About the Program

Mr. Vargas went to school at Louisiana State University in Shreveport achieving a Bachelors degree in Mathematics and Physics with emphasis in Mathematics and a Minor in Computer Science in 2016. He is currently working on his Master's Degree at the University of Texas at Dallas. He has experience programming with Java, JavaScript, Python, iOS, HTML, CSS, and scratch. Mr. Vargas believes that learning how to use an engineering mindset to solve problems - of any type - is extremely useful. The ability to break down problems into smaller components is the way to complete any task. The journey of a thousand miles begins with the first step.







My Teaching Journey

I personally have been teaching for many years. It all began while I tutored Mathematics - Algebra II, Pre Calculus and Calculus - in high school. I then continued to tutor through college. While in college I also began to teach martial arts. This is trully what showed me the joys of teaching. Then I started teaching in a school district, it all began here at West in 2017. My first year, I was teaching the Algebra I and 8th grade robotics. From then on, I continued to teach Mathematics while overseeing the growth of the Robotics Magnet. I currently teach the entirety of the robotics Magnet, and always look for ways to improve and better my program. In 2020 I was awarded the TI STEM award for the use of technology in our classroom.
